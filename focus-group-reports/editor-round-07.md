# Editor Round 7 — Voice & Soul

## VERDICT: Voice is working. Craft needs refinement.

### What works:
- "Cool reporter in a room with maps" — consistent, calm, precise
- Opening impact: generators humming in Tehran is observable and grounding
- Facts carry weight without editorializing
- Attribution sits in right place

### What needs work:
1. **Story order is chronological, not thematic** — reader sees parallel crises but not HOW they connect. The Iran→Cuba→Kenya thread (fuel→sanctions→port closures) is invisible.
2. **Consequence layer mixes timeframes** — Cuba's 3-month embargo is presented as same-day as plant failure. Need temporal separation.
3. **Implicit sourcing gaps** — "Houthi forces fired missiles" with no attribution
4. **Numbers without precision tags** — "$800K/day losses" — confirmed or estimated?
5. **Architectural editorializing** — story order implies hierarchy without stating it

## KEY FIX: Story order guidance in the prompt (thematic grouping, not chronological) + temporal separation rule for old conditions vs. new triggers.
